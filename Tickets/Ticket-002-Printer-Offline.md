# Ticket 002 - Printer Offline

## Problem

A user reported that they could not print documents and the printer showed as offline.

## What I Checked

I confirmed the printer was powered on and connected to the network.

## Troubleshooting Steps

- Restarted the printer
- Checked network connectivity
- Cleared the print queue
- Sent a test page

## Resolution

The printer came back online after restarting and clearing the queue.

The test page printed successfully.

## What I Learned

Sometimes print jobs get stuck and prevent other jobs from processing correctly.
