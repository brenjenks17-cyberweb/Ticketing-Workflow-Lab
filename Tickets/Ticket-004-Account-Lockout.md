# Ticket 004 - Account Lockout

## Problem

A user contacted support because they were locked out of their account and couldn't sign in.

## What I Checked

I verified the user's identity and checked the account status. The account showed as locked after several failed login attempts.

## Troubleshooting Steps

* Verified the user's identity
* Confirmed the account was locked
* Unlocked the account
* Reset the password
* Had the user test the new password

## Resolution

The user was able to log in successfully after the account was unlocked and the password was reset.

## What I Learned

This reminded me that lockouts aren't always caused by someone forgetting their password. Sometimes an old password is saved on another device and keeps trying to sign in.
